Explore the Royal City

Mysore Palace

An iconic royal residence known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture and grand interiors.

Chamundi Hills

A sacred hill offering panoramic views of the city and home to the Chamundeshwari Temple.

Brindavan Gardens

Famous for its musical fountain and beautiful symmetrical landscaping near Krishnarajasagara Dam.

Mysore Zoo

One of India's oldest and most well-maintained zoos, known for its diverse wildlife.

St. Philomena's Church

A stunning Neo-Gothic church, one of the tallest in Asia, dedicated to Saint Philomena.

Jaganmohan Palace and Art Gallery

A historic palace turned museum featuring royal artifacts and traditional Mysore paintings.

Karanji Lake

A tranquil lake with a butterfly park and boating, perfect for nature lovers and bird watchers.

Railway Museum

Showcases vintage locomotives and exhibits the history of Indian Railways in South India.

Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary

A scenic bird sanctuary on the banks of the Cauvery River, home to migratory birds and boat rides through lush greenery.

Lalitha Mahal Palace

A majestic white palace built for royal guests, now a heritage hotel known for its European-style architecture and elegance.

Kukkarahalli Lake

A peaceful urban lake surrounded by walking trails and greenery, popular for bird watching, jogging, and enjoying serene sunsets in the heart of Mysuru.
